Flux is the official physics club of DPS Dwarka

About us

Flux, the physics club of DPS, Dwarka was started in 2015 with the aim of providing students with an insight into the vast world of physics. The club was intiated by DR. Umesh Chhikara, with the full support of the entire physics department, under the tutelage our respected Principal, Madam Sunita Tanwar.

Starting from simple workshops to a spectrum of different events at intra and inter-school level, Flux has shown remarkable progress towards encouraging students to strive for excellence. The club gives the students a platform ro identify their talents and pursue them. Last year in collaboration with Genesis, Ionista and Axiom, the second edition of IGNITE AND AXIOM- the inter-school science and maths symposium was conducted which turned out to be an enormous success.

EVENTS

"Electricity is just organized lightning" - George Carlin
Do you think you have what it takes to control electricity? Come find out at Circuitron, an event to test the knowledge, both practical and theoretical, in the field of circuits. Participating teams will have to use wires, capacitors, resistors etc. to build a circuit on a breadboard.
"Ignorance is temporary affliction, remedied by only by asking the right questions."
Unleash your inner genius at the FLux Quiz. The mentally stimulating questions will put the gears of problem solving into motion.
"The heritage of mankind is not the Earth, but the universe."
Every great mind has sought to have knowledge of the universe. Interstellar involves not only the field of astrology, but also robotics. The participating teams will have to maneuver over the undisclosed terrain of some foreign planet. A question will be posed to them after crossing each obstacle. Come and shoot for the stars at interstellar.
"Arguments aim at finding out who is right. A discussion is to determine what is right."
The future lies in the hands of the youth. The way to make it fruitful is to regonise the various issues and finding a solution. The Group Discission event at Flux encourages the students ti brainstornm over the prevalent topics in the field of Science and express their take/view on them.
"To most people the sky is the limit. To those who love aviation, the sly is home."
Aquajet tests the aptitude of the students in the fields of aerodynamics and practical physics. The participants will be required to build a water propelled rocket. The judgement will be based on the ditance and height obtained. It's literal rocket science.
